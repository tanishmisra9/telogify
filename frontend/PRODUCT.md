# Telogify (frontend)

F1 telemetry intelligence. Three surfaces, deliberately minimal:

1. **Home / weekends list** — processed race weekends, each linking to its page.
2. **Race weekend page** — the hero. 3 quantified insights at the top (telemetry numbers
   emphasised), constructor + driver pace plots (Recharts), finishing order.
3. **Subscribe** — email capture, tagline "Let's telogify this weekend".

register: product (the design serves the data; insights are the product)

Audience: F1 fans and analysts reading weekend takeaways on a laptop, focused, data-forward.
The numbers are the point; the UI gets out of their way.
